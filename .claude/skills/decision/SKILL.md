# /decision — Record a Decision

Append a methodological decision to DECISION-LOG.md.

## Steps

1. Ask: What decision was made?
2. Ask: What prompted this decision?
3. Ask: What alternatives were considered?
4. Ask: Is this an override of a craft recommendation? (Yes/No)
5. Append the entry to DECISION-LOG.md with today's date
6. Confirm the entry was recorded
